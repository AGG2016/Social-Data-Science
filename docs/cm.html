Main Message: Correlation vs. Causation

  • Correlation does not mean causation
  • Conservative wording includes: association,influence, variables appear to move together…
  • Simple correlation cannot be stated for multivariate regression
  • Confounding variables not including can explain the association
  • Time is often a natural confounder, which likely leads to spurious correlation
  • Space can be also a natural confounder; for instance, population influences many phenomena, so it must be properly controlled
  • If there is plausible causal mechanism, correlation is simply due to chance
  • It is important to be cautios with pathological examples
